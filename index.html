Our Story

A patisserie that speaks two languages.

At Saisha's, classical French pâtisserie meets the restraint and precision of Japanese confectionery. Two traditions, neither diluted, in every croissant, tart, and cake we make.

Everything is handcrafted in our atelier on Piccadilly Street. Small batches. Fresh daily. We'd rather sell out by afternoon than compromise on craft.
