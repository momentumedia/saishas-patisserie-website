Our Story

Made with intention

A patisserie on Piccadilly Street. Two culinary traditions. One small team.

Saisha's
The Beginning

French craft, Japanese precision.

A small atelier on Piccadilly Street where classical French pâtisserie meets the restraint of Japanese confectionery. Everything is handcrafted in small batches, fresh daily. When something sells out, it sells out.

What We Believe

Three principles, every single day.

🌸

Fusion without compromise

French technique is followed to the letter. Japanese aesthetics are honoured fully. Neither is watered down.

🥐

Small batches, always

Fresh every day in small quantities. Never reheated. Never frozen.

🎂

Every ingredient matters

Valrhona chocolate. Hokkaido matcha. Frantoia olive oil. Real butter. We don't cut corners on ingredients.
