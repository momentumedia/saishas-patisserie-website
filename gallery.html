The Gallery

A visual journal

Photographs from the atelier. Our cakes, viennoiseries, seasonal chocolate, and signature packaging.

Every cake at Saisha's has a name and a story. Every croissant is laminated by hand. Every tart is finished the morning it's served. These are the photographs that document that care. Hover any image for its name, click to enlarge.

The Signatures

Layered mousse cakes and composed desserts. Each one a small composition of flavour, texture, and restraint.

The Viennoiseries

Butter, dough, and patience. Hand-laminated croissants baked fresh each morning in small batches.

The Tarts

Pâte sablée and sucrée, blind-baked, filled with seasonal cream and fruit. Simple in structure, careful in execution.

Whole Cakes

Full-size versions of our signature cakes, for gatherings, birthdays, and celebrations. All pre-order.
